# High Concept

## One sentence

A multiplayer political sandbox where royal heirs compete to become monarch after their father's death through trust, deception, legitimacy, public support, wealth, and violence.

## Player fantasy

> “I am a member of the royal family fighting for the throne.”

The player is not merely surviving. They build a claim, protect it, and decide who to trust while every other claimant does the same.

| Item | Direction |
|---|---|
| Genre | Multiplayer Social Strategy / PvPvE Sandbox |
| Players | 4–8 |
| Camera | Third person |
| Engine | Unreal Engine 5 |
| Session goal | Establish a supported path to the throne before a rival does |

## Inspirations

- Rust: embodied multiplayer tension
- Among Us: suspicion and incomplete information
- Crusader Kings: ambition and factions
- Kingdom Come: grounded medieval atmosphere

# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation

- validate high concept and design pillars
- decide MVP victory condition and death-state approach
- create palace greybox
- establish networked movement and council flow

## Phase 1 — Playable political loop

- council voting
- core power resources
- basic relationships
- private/public interaction spaces
- first social playtests

## Phase 2 — Conflict loop

- three assassination methods
- evidence generation and trial flow
- limited NPC reports and rumors
- test counterplay, false accusations, and recovery paths

## Phase 3 — Vertical slice

- polish palace route design
- finalize a complete match arc
- add result recap and telemetry
- tune balance from playtest data

## Open decisions

- exact victory thresholds and tie-breakers
- post-death player experience
- role selection versus random assignment
- in-game versus external voice
- moderation and accessibility requirements

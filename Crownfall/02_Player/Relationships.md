# Player Relationships

Personal relationships make faction-level politics social. Every player has a separate hidden relationship value with every other player.

| Range | Label | Default behavior |
|---:|---|---|
| 60 to 100 | Trusted | shares risk and information more readily |
| 20 to 59 | Cooperative | accepts limited deals |
| -19 to 19 | Uncertain | requires proof or incentive |
| -59 to -20 | Hostile | resists and spreads counter-narratives |
| -100 to -60 | Vengeful | actively seeks punishment or removal |

Promises, votes, gifts, public support, betrayal, accusations, and violence change relationships. The exact number is hidden; players infer it through behavior and reports.

## Rules

- Relationships are not contracts: trusted allies can betray.
- Public and secret actions can affect relationships differently.
- Faction support cannot replace personal trust.

# Match Flow

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Lobby] --> B[Funeral: throne vacant]
    B --> C[Early political positioning]
    C --> D[First major incident]
    D --> E[Investigation and alliances]
    E --> F[Power crisis]
    F --> G[Succession outcome]
    G --> H[Results and story recap]
```

## Phases

1. **Lobby (2–3 min):** players receive or choose a royal-heir identity; differences guide playstyle but do not determine a winner.
2. **Funeral:** the king is dead and no heir is declared; every player is a claimant.
3. **Early positioning:** first council decision reveals priorities, then players seek allies, favors, wealth, and information.
4. **Crisis:** murder, scandal, policy failure, rebellion, or external event destabilizes alliances.
5. **Final succession:** a claimant completes a valid victory route or removes all viable opposing claims.

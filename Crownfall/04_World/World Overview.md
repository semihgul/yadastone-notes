# World Overview

## Palace — MVP play space

- throne room
- dining hall
- secret passages
- dungeon
- towers
- garden
- armory
- treasury
- chapel

The palace supports council meetings, surveillance, private negotiations, restricted access, and assassination opportunities.

## Post-MVP regions

**City:** market, town square, tavern, harbor, guilds, poor district.

**Kingdom:** villages, castles, mines, forests, borderlands.

## World-design rule

Every location must support at least one of: political visibility, private intrigue, resource conversion, information gathering, or risk.

# Assassination

## Design goal

Assassination is high-risk political disruption. It must be threatening but neither trivial nor the only optimal route to victory.

## MVP methods

- bribe or distract a guard to use a restricted route
- poison food or drink through a compromised servant or chef
- use darkness or a diversion for a close-range dagger attack

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Choose target and method] --> B[Acquire access, item, or accomplice]
    B --> C[Create opportunity]
    C --> D[Attempt]
    D --> E[Escape, conceal, or frame]
    E --> F[Evidence and political fallout]
```

## Rules

- Every method requires preparation and leaves a counterplay window.
- Killing a rival does not guarantee progress toward the throne.
- A failed attempt can still create evidence, fear, suspicion, injury, or faction loss.
- Targets have prevention options: guards, escorts, trust networks, changed routines, and public exposure.

# MVP Scope

## Question to validate

> Is accusing, lying, manipulating, and politically outplaying other players genuinely fun?

## Included

- palace map only
- 4–8 player multiplayer session
- royal council and voting
- basic proximity communication
- basic resource and faction feedback
- limited assassination methods
- basic evidence and trial flow
- limited NPC gossip
- one or more clear succession outcomes

## Not included

- city and kingdom regions
- deep economy and guilds
- advanced AI simulation
- broad spy-network system
- tunnel construction
- expanded religion mechanics
- seasons, weather, and large-scale war

## Exit criteria

The vertical slice succeeds if playtests repeatedly create understandable, player-driven stories of cooperation, accusation, betrayal, and competing paths to power.
